Pav Bhaji Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, cauliflower, peas, carrots), boiled and mashed
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, chopped
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 pav (dinner rolls)
  • Chopped coriander leaves for garnishing
  • Lemon wedges

Instructions

  1. In a large pan, melt the butter and saut√© the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the chopped bell peppers and cook for a few minutes until they soften.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes, pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn mushy.
  4. Add the boiled and mashed vegetables. Mix well and let it cook for about 10 minutes, mashing and stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, slit the pav horizontally and lightly toast them on a griddle with a little butter.
  6. Once the bhaji is cooked and has a thick consistency, turn off the heat.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  8. Serve the hot bhaji with toasted pav, lemon wedges, and additional chopped onions if desired.
