Veg Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, peas, potatoes), chopped
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon Biryani masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 4-6 whole cloves
  • 4-6 whole green cardamom pods
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk (for garnishing)

Instructions

  1. Wash the Basmati rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for about 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee. Add cumin seeds, cloves, green cardamom, and bay leaves. Sauté for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
  4. Add ginger paste and garlic paste. Sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, Biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft and the oil starts to separate.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Whisk yogurt and add it to the pot. Mix well.
  8. Add chopped mint and coriander leaves.
  9. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and add the soaked and drained rice. Cook the rice until it's 70-80% cooked. Drain the rice.
  10. Layer the partially cooked rice over the vegetable mixture in the pot.
  11. Garnish the rice with saffron-soaked milk and a few mint leaves.
  12. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes. This allows the rice to steam and cook fully.
  13. Once done, gently fluff the rice with a fork.
  14. Serve the flavorful Veg Biryani with raita, salad, or your favorite side dish.
