Veg Grilled Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 1 large potato, boiled and thinly sliced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup boiled sweet corn
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Butter for spreading
  • Green chutney or mayonnaise for spreading

Instructions

  1. Prepare all the sliced vegetables and keep them ready.
  2. Mix the grated carrot, bell peppers, boiled sweet corn, chaat masala, black pepper powder, and salt in a bowl to create the filling mixture.
  3. Take a slice of bread and spread butter on one side. Flip it over and spread green chutney or mayonnaise on the other side.
  4. Place a layer of potato slices on the buttered side, followed by a layer of onion, tomato, and the mixed vegetable filling.
  5. Cover the sandwich with another slice of bread.
  6. Preheat a sandwich maker or a grill pan.
  7. Place the prepared sandwich in the sandwich maker or grill pan and cook until the bread turns golden brown and crispy.
  8. Remove from the grill, cut the sandwich into halves, and serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
