Chocolate Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream
  • Chocolate shavings or sprinkles for garnishing

Instructions

  1. Add the vanilla ice cream, milk, and chocolate syrup to a blender.
  2. Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour the chocolate milkshake into serving glasses.
  4. Top each glass with a dollop of whipped cream.
  5. Garnish with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.
  6. Insert a straw and serve the delicious chocolate milkshake immediately.
