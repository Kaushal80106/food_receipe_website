Pani Puri

Ingredients

  • 30-40 puris (hollow, crispy shells)
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh coriander leaves
  • 1/4 cup tamarind chutney
  • For the Pani (Spiced Water):
  • 1 cup mint leaves
  • 1/2 cup coriander leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon black salt
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups cold water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pani by blending mint leaves, coriander leaves, green chilies, ginger paste, cumin powder, chaat masala, black salt, regular salt, and cold water in a blender. Strain the mixture to remove any solids.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine boiled and mashed potatoes, boiled chickpeas, chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix well.
  3. To serve, carefully make a small hole in each puri to create a pocket.
  4. Fill each puri with the potato and chickpea mixture.
  5. Add a little tamarind chutney to each puri.
  6. Immerse each filled puri into the prepared spiced water (pani).
  7. Consume the entire puri in one bite to experience the burst of flavors.
  8. Repeat the process with the remaining puris.
  9. Enjoy the refreshing and tangy Pani Puri!
